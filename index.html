About the Project

Founded in 1982, Supreme Universal has been driven by a singular passion — Commitment to Excellence, Elegance & Sophistication. We revolutionised luxury living with 3 BHK Townhouses in Somatane, and now raise the bar further with exclusive 4 BHK Townhouses and Twin Villas.

Supreme Villagio is a low-density villa-style development with modern architecture featuring large decks, open layouts, and thoughtfully curated amenities. The grand 3.5-acre central Boulevard Landscape has been designed by Mark Mahan, Singapore-based Design Director at Site Concepts.

Somatane — once Pune's quiet hamlet — is now a rising destination with significant growth, near-zero noise pollution, better AQI than the city, and at the same elevation as Lonavala. Splendid uninterrupted views of hills and endless greens await you.

The Villagio Difference

Where Architecture Meets Soul

Supreme Villagio redefines what it means to call a place home. With villa-style architecture, expansive open floor plans, and seamlessly connected indoor-outdoor living, every townhouse and villa is a private sanctuary.

The project spans lush green land where 65% is dedicated to open spaces, gardens, and community areas. The central 3.5-acre Boulevard, designed by Singapore's Site Concepts, ties the entire community together in a ribbon of green luxury.

With Sahyadri views, Lonavala-level elevation, and superior AQI compared to city life, you're not just buying a home — you're buying a way of life.
